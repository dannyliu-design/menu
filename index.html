Cocktail Selection

Koko Head

Collins Glass
  • 1½oz Malibu Coconut Rum
  • Fresh Lime & Mint Leaves
  • Coconut Water & Club Soda

Method

Muddle lime/mint. Add rum & water, toss and top with soda. Garnish with mint.

Old Fut

10oz Gibraltar
  • 2oz Bulleit Bourbon
  • ½oz Bar Syrup
  • 2 dashes Bitters
  • Splash of Water

Method

Stir ingredients with ice ~40 times. Strain over fresh ice. Top with water.

P.O.G.

Coupe Glass
  • 1½oz Malibu Coconut Rum
  • Lilikoi Syrup & Guava Juice
  • Orange Juice & Coconut Water

Method

Combine all, shake with ice, and strain into coupe. Garnish with Mint Sprig.

Guava Mule

Copper Mug
  • 1½oz Absolut Vodka
  • Strawberries & Lime Wedges
  • Guava Juice & Ginger Beer

Method

Muddle fruit. Shake vodka/syrup. Strain over ice. Top with juice & ginger beer.

No Can

Non-Alcoholic
  • 2oz Coconut Water
  • 2oz Pineapple & Guava Juice
  • Fresh Squeezed Lime

Method

Combine in 14oz glass with ice. Garnish with mint & lime on a pick.

Lucky Sunset

14oz Glass
  • 2oz 1800 Silver Tequila
  • Triple Sec & Lilikoi Syrup
  • Pineapple Juice | Li Hing Mui Rim

Method

Shake and strain over fresh ice. Serve in a glass with a Li Hing rim.

Mai Tai

14oz Glass
  • Old Lahaina Silver & Dark Rum
  • Orange Curacao | Orgeat
  • Lime, Pineapple & Orange Juice

Method

Shake all but dark rum. Strain. Float dark rum on top. Garnish with pineapple.

Naked Kane

10oz Gibraltar
  • 1½oz Bombay Sapphire Gin
  • Agave & Lemon Juice
  • Fresh Mint & Cucumber

Method

Muddle mint/cucumber. Shake gin, agave, lemon. Top with soda.

Margarita

14oz Glass
  • 2oz 1800 Silver Tequila
  • ½oz Cointreau Liqueur
  • Lime Sour Mix | Fresh Lime

Method

Shake & strain. Salt rim on request. Garnish with lime wedge.
